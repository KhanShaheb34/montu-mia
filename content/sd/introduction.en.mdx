Montu Mia is a software engineer, working away happily at a fairly well-known company. But one day, out of nowhere, a 'billion dollar' idea struck him. He would build an app called **'BiralTube' (BiralTube)** — a place where people share all sorts of cute cat videos and, before you know it, take the world by storm.

No sooner thought than done! Working day and night, Montu had the app ready in just two months. The moment he published it — a hit! Thousands of people came rushing to use BiralTube. Montu was over the moon! Sitting at his desk, he had already started daydreaming about flinging a 'Resignation Letter' right in his boss's face.

But this happiness was not meant to last. Within just a few days, all sorts of ghostly problems began creeping into Montu Mia's app. Users started complaining — for some the app ran slow, for others videos would not load, and when someone hit "like" it would not get counted. One by one, a thousand problems closed in around Montu.

![Expectation vs Reality](/images/sd/introduction/1.png)

Poor Montu was thrown into deep worry. How do the world's giant software systems (like Facebook and Google) run with hundreds of millions of users? And why was his humble BiralTube already gasping for breath?

Out of options, Montu went to his office's senior 'guru', Boltu Mia. After hearing the whole story, Boltu Mia smiled and said, **"Nephew, you wrote the code — but did you learn 'System Design'? Without that, your app is bound to collapse!"**

And that was it! On Boltu Mia's advice, Montu Mia rolled up his sleeves and got down to studying. At the same time, fixing his BiralTube carried on too. Right from the very beginning, he started learning all the heavyweight concepts of system design — scaling, load balancing, partitioning, CDN, everything — hands-on. And like magic, his app began running at rocket speed once again.

You too will join Montu Mia on this adventure. We will watch what problems Montu Mia runs into, and how he comes up with smart solutions to them using the knowledge of system design.
